Welcome to Finland


ParqueTravels offers trips to three different locations: Lapland, Helsinki and Tampere.

Lapland


In Lapland you can experience exciting adventures and enjoy a truly beautiful landscape.

Hike on the mountains, see reindeers, try husky rides and experience winter fun!

5 days in Lapland, all-inclusive accommodation in Hotel Rovaniemi: 390€.

Helsinki


In Helsinki you can experience the lively life in the capital city of Finland.

Go shopping in various malls, see beautiful architecture and explore culture!

Tampere


In Tampere you can experience life between two great lakes and explore the city with its interesting history.

Visit the museum, swim in Lake Näsijärvi and Lake Pyhäjärvi or taste black sausage!

7 days in Tampere, all-inclusive accommodation in Hotel Tampere: 480.
