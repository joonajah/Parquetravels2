Welcome to Finland


ParqueTravels offers trips to three different locations: Hervanta, Korso and Forssa.

Hervanta


In Hervanta you can experience an exciting adventure and see beautiful architecture.

Swim in Suolijärvi, buy souvenirs in the mall Duo or have a refreshing drink in Amazonas!

4 days in Hervanta, accommodation in Sähkökatu: 180€. Food not included.

One week in Hervanta, accommodation in Teräskatu: 230€. Food not included.

Book a trip to Hervanta


Korso


In Korso you can see the most beautiful side of Vantaa.

Visit the library or the church, arrange your personal business in Kela, or just have fun!

4 days in Korso, accommodation in Korson VPK: 150€. Food not included.

One week in Korso, accommodation in Maarukanmetsä: 210€. Food not included.

Book a trip to Korso

Forssa


In Forssa you can visit many parks and talk about cars with local teenagers.

Visit the museum, church, Pilvenmäen ravirata or race with your friends at the go-kart track!

4 days in Forssa, accommodation in Ypäjän Hevosopisto: 550€. Food is included.

One week in Forssa, accommodation in Änkyräperä: 780€. Food is included (gluten free).

Book a trip to Forssa
