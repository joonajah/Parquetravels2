Welcome to Norway


ParqueTravels offers trips to two of the largest and most beautiful cities in Norway: Oslo and Bergen

Oslo


The capital and the largest city of Norway has beautiful architecture and fun activies for all ages.

Visit various museums such as the Viking Ship Museum and have a fun day at the Tusenfryd amusement park!

4 day compact trip in Oslo at the Parque Hotel: 350€/person.

7 day relaxing trip in Oslo at the Parque Hotel: 550€/person.

Book your trip here!


Bergen


The wonderful city of the seven mountains is located on the west coast of Norway.

Visit the mountain of Fløyen via Fløibanen and experience the lively atmosphere of Bergen Fish Market.

4 day compact trip in Bergen at the Laminate Hotel: 300€/person.

7 day relaxing trip in Bergen at the Laminate Hotel: 450€/person.
