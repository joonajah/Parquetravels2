Welcome to Russia


ParqueTravels offers trips to 3 places in Russia:

Moscow, St. Petersburg and Siberia.


Moscow


The capital and the largest city of Russia has beautiful architecture and fun activies for all ages.

Visit various places such as St. Bails's Cathedral, Lenin's Mausoleum, the Moscow Kremlin, the State Historical Museum.

Other places to visit also are GUM department store, Arbat street, Tretyakov Gallery and the Pushkin State Museum of Fine Arts.

Go listen to the Russia's underground Hard Bass scene, where musicians are pushing the boundaries in beats.

At the Gorky Park you can practice your gopnik slavic squats.

4 day compact trip in Moscow at the Ritz-Carlton Hotel: 350€/person.

7 day relaxing trip in Moscow at the Ritz-Carlton Hotel: 550€/person.

St. Petersburg


Sain Petersburg is called the world's cultural capital.

Go visit the Hermitage, St. Isaac's Cathedral, Russian National Library and the Russian Museum.

The Hermitage is the second-largest museum in the world.

There are over 300 bridges in St. Petersburg. There is a large underground system in the city.

The famous football club Zenit is a multiple winner of national championships, and a winner of EUFA Cup and Super Cup.

Siberia


Try the Trans-Siberian Railway to get to your destination. Go hike in the Altai mountains.

The Trans-Siberian Railway is the longest rail road int he world, with 9289 kilometers of rails.

Lake Baikal is the must-see if you are planning to go to Siberia. Lake Baikal is the deepest lake in the world.
