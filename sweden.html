Welcome to Sweden


ParqueTravels offers trips to two different locations: Stockholm and Gävle.

Stockholm


In the capital of Sweden you can dive right in to the Swedish culture.

Have a nice cup of Löfbergs and a rye-bread with surströmming.

4 days in Stockholm, accommodation in Stockholm metro-station: 30€. Food not included.

One week in Stockholm, accommodation in front of Stockholm Concert Hall: 75€. Food not included.

Book a trip to Stockholm

Gävle


Gävle is the 13th most populated city in Sweden.

Its most well known sight is the notorious Gävlebocken, a giant bock made from hay that is burned every year.

4 days in Gävle, accommodation beneath the burnt bock: 20€. Food not included.

One week in Gävle, accommodation in Gävle prison (in the picture) and museum: 175€. Food included.
