In the capital of Sweden you can dive right in to the Swedish culture.

Have a nice cup of Löfbergs and a rye-bread with surströmming.

Gävle


Gävle is the 13th most populated city in Sweden.

Its most well known sight is the notorious Gävlebocken, a giant statue made from hay that is burned almost every year.

4 days in Gävle, accommodation in Hotel Gävle: 220€. Food not included.

7 days in Gävle, accommodation in Hotel Gävle: 350€. Food included.
